Makerspace Club 2025 Competitions

YIC (Young Innovators Challenge)

Criteria: 13-17 years old, 2-3 students per team

Challenge: "Help a person, change the world" – Identify a real problem and propose 1 solution.

Submission: Prototype with Arduino & Pitching slide

Due Date: 31 July 2024 (Mentoring provided)

Prize: Certificate, chance to join state Maker Fair, Medals

Cost: Registration is free; prototype components may cost RM30 per group

Visit YIC Website
NGFP (Young Voices Awards)

Criteria: 13-17 years old, 2-3 students per team

Challenge: Create a positive change in your community – Focus on 1 SDG Goal

Submission: Short essay (400 words) & Video (2 min 30 sec)

Due Date: 30 June 2024 (Workshops provided)

Prize: Certificate and cash prize for winners

Cost: Registration is free

Visit NGFP Website
NAIC (National AI Competition)

Criteria: Team of 4 students (Category A: Form 3/Year 9 – Form 5/Year 11)

Challenge: AI Art: Create an A3 size artwork using AI tools.
AI Innovation: Propose AI solutions for real-world problems.
AI Technical: Develop AI models with hands-on coding to solve real-world challenges.

Submission: AI Art: Final artwork plus a writeup on theme reflection and creative process.
AI Innovation: Writeup on the problem, AI solution, social impact, and a prototype (app mockup/model images).
AI Technical: Complete codebase with a methodology writeup.

Due Date: Preliminary (Online Submission) – Deadline: 24 May; Final Round – Physical presentation at Sunway University for shortlisted participants

Prize: Cash, certificate, visit to Google office, and 6 months of premium photo editing

Cost: Free of charge

Visit NAIC Website
